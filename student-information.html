Comprehensive Student Management

Student Profiles

Maintain comprehensive digital profiles with demographics, contact information, emergency contacts, medical records, and custom fields tailored to your needs.

Academic Records

Track grades, transcripts, course history, GPA calculations, and academic achievements. Generate report cards and progress reports automatically.

Attendance Tracking

Record daily attendance with biometric integration, RFID, or manual entry. Set automated alerts for absences and generate attendance reports.

Health & Medical Records

Store vaccination records, allergies, medical conditions, and visit logs. Ensure staff can access critical health information when needed.

Behavioral Management

Log disciplinary actions, positive reinforcements, and behavioral incidents. Track patterns and intervene early with data-driven insights.

Parent Communication

Enable parents to view student progress, attendance, and grades through a dedicated portal. Send instant notifications and announcements.
